Geometry Formula Library

A compact reference of common formulas for a high school Geometry course.

1. Points, Lines, and Coordinate Geometry

Distance Formula

\[d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}\]

Midpoint Formula

\[M = \left(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right)\]

Slope Formula

\[m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}\]

Slope-Intercept Form

\[y = mx + b\]

Point-Slope Form

\[y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)\]

Standard Form of a Linear Equation

\[Ax + By = C\]

Parallel Lines

Parallel lines have equal slopes:

\[m_1 = m_2\]

Perpendicular Lines

Perpendicular lines have slopes that are negative reciprocals:

\[m_1 \cdot m_2 = -1\]

2. Angle Relationships

Complementary Angles

\[a + b = 90^\circ\]

Supplementary Angles

\[a + b = 180^\circ\]

Linear Pair

\[a + b = 180^\circ\]

Vertical Angles

Vertical angles are congruent:

\[a = b\]

3. Triangles

Triangle Angle Sum

\[a + b + c = 180^\circ\]

Exterior Angle Theorem

\[\text{Exterior Angle} = \text{Remote Interior Angle}_1 + \text{Remote Interior Angle}_2\]

Area of a Triangle

\[A = \frac{1}{2}bh\]

Pythagorean Theorem

\[a^2 + b^2 = c^2\]

Converse of the Pythagorean Theorem

If

\[a^2 + b^2 = c^2\]

then the triangle is a right triangle.

45-45-90 Triangle

Side ratio:

\[x : x : x\sqrt{2}\]

30-60-90 Triangle

Side ratio:

\[x : x\sqrt{3} : 2x\]

Heron's Formula

Semiperimeter:

\[s = \frac{a + b + c}{2}\]

Area:

\[A = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}\]

4. Triangle Congruence and Similarity

Congruent Triangles

Common congruence shortcuts:

\[SSS,\ SAS,\ ASA,\ AAS,\ HL\]

Similar Triangles

Common similarity shortcuts:

\[AA,\ SSS,\ SAS\]

Scale Factor

\[k = \frac{\text{new length}}{\text{original length}}\]

Perimeter Scale Factor

\[\frac{P_2}{P_1} = k\]

Area Scale Factor

\[\frac{A_2}{A_1} = k^2\]

Volume Scale Factor

\[\frac{V_2}{V_1} = k^3\]

5. Polygons

Sum of Interior Angles

For an \(n\)-sided polygon:

\[S = 180(n - 2)\]

One Interior Angle of a Regular Polygon

\[\text{Interior Angle} = \frac{180(n - 2)}{n}\]

Sum of Exterior Angles

\[S = 360^\circ\]

One Exterior Angle of a Regular Polygon

\[\text{Exterior Angle} = \frac{360}{n}\]

Number of Diagonals

\[d = \frac{n(n - 3)}{2}\]

6. Quadrilaterals

Area of a Rectangle

\[A = lw\]

Perimeter of a Rectangle

\[P = 2l + 2w\]

Area of a Square

\[A = s^2\]

Perimeter of a Square

\[P = 4s\]

Area of a Parallelogram

\[A = bh\]

Area of a Trapezoid

\[A = \frac{1}{2}(b_1 + b_2)h\]

Area of a Rhombus or Kite

\[A = \frac{1}{2}d_1d_2\]

7. Circles

Circumference

\[C = 2\pi r\]

or

\[C = \pi d\]

Area of a Circle

\[A = \pi r^2\]

Arc Length

\[L = \frac{\theta}{360^\circ} \cdot 2\pi r\]

Sector Area

\[A = \frac{\theta}{360^\circ} \cdot \pi r^2\]

Equation of a Circle

Center \((h,k)\), radius \(r\):

\[(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2\]

Circle Theorem: Central Angle and Arc

\[m\angle AOB = m\overset{\frown}{AB}\]

Circle Theorem: Inscribed Angle

\[m\angle ABC = \frac{1}{2}m\overset{\frown}{AC}\]

Tangent-Radius Theorem

A tangent line is perpendicular to the radius at the point of tangency:

\[\text{tangent} \perp \text{radius}\]

8. Right Triangle Trigonometry

Sine

\[\sin(\theta) = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}}\]

Cosine

\[\cos(\theta) = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}}\]

Tangent

\[\tan(\theta) = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}}\]

SOH-CAH-TOA

\[SOH \quad CAH \quad TOA\]

Angle of Elevation / Depression

Often modeled with:

\[\tan(\theta) = \frac{\text{vertical change}}{\text{horizontal distance}}\]

9. Transformations

Translation

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (x + a, y + b)\]

Reflection Across the x-axis

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (x, -y)\]

Reflection Across the y-axis

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, y)\]

Reflection Across the Line \(y = x\)

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (y, x)\]

Reflection Across the Line \(y = -x\)

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (-y, -x)\]

Rotation 90° Counterclockwise About the Origin

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (-y, x)\]

Rotation 180° About the Origin

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, -y)\]

Rotation 270° Counterclockwise About the Origin

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (y, -x)\]

Dilation About the Origin

\[(x, y) \rightarrow (kx, ky)\]

10. Three-Dimensional Geometry

Volume of a Rectangular Prism

\[V = lwh\]

Surface Area of a Rectangular Prism

\[SA = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh\]

Volume of a Cube

\[V = s^3\]

Surface Area of a Cube

\[SA = 6s^2\]

Volume of a Cylinder

\[V = \pi r^2h\]

Surface Area of a Cylinder

\[SA = 2\pi r^2 + 2\pi rh\]

Volume of a Cone

\[V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2h\]

Surface Area of a Cone

\[SA = \pi r^2 + \pi r\ell\]

Volume of a Sphere

\[V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\]

Surface Area of a Sphere

\[SA = 4\pi r^2\]

Volume of a Pyramid

\[V = \frac{1}{3}Bh\]

Volume of a Prism

\[V = Bh\]

Surface Area of a Prism

\[SA = 2B + Ph\]

where \(B\) is the area of the base and \(P\) is the perimeter of the base.

11. Density and Applied Geometry

Density

\[D = \frac{m}{V}\]

Percent Error

\[\text{Percent Error} = \frac{|\text{measured} - \text{actual}|}{\text{actual}} \times 100\%\]

12. Algebra Formulas Often Used in Geometry

Quadratic Formula

\[x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\]

Difference of Squares

\[a^2 - b^2 = (a - b)(a + b)\]

Perfect Square Trinomial

\[a^2 + 2ab + b^2 = (a + b)^2\]
\[a^2 - 2ab + b^2 = (a - b)^2\]

Solving a Proportion

\[\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d}\]

Cross multiply:

\[ad = bc\]
